Fathom Notetaker for Windows Team

Transform your team meetings with AI-powered recording, transcription, and note-taking

What is Fathom Notetaker?

Fathom is an AI-powered tool that records, transcribes, highlights, and summarizes your meetings so you can focus on the conversation rather than taking notes.

Recording

Automatically records your meetings with high-quality audio and video.

Transcription

Provides accurate, real-time transcription of your meetings.

Highlighting

Allows you to mark important moments during the meeting.

Summarization

Generates concise summaries of your meetings automatically.

Why Use Fathom in Your Windows Team?

Fathom offers numerous benefits that can enhance productivity and collaboration within your Windows Team environment:

  • Increased Focus: Team members can actively participate in discussions without worrying about taking notes.
  • Accurate Records: Eliminates the problem of incomplete or inaccurate meeting notes.
  • Accessibility: Team members who missed the meeting can quickly catch up with recordings and summaries.
  • Actionable Insights: AI highlights key decisions and action items for follow-up.
  • Time Savings: Reduces time spent on meeting documentation and follow-up communications.

Using Fathom During Meetings

Once installed, Fathom seamlessly integrates with your meeting workflow:

Before the Meeting

  • Fathom automatically detects meetings from your connected calendar
  • Configure settings for each meeting (recording preferences, language, etc.)
  • Invite Fathom to your meeting if it's not automatically added

During the Meeting

  • Fathom automatically starts recording when the meeting begins
  • Use the Fathom widget to highlight important moments in real-time
  • Add private notes that only you can see
  • View live transcription as the meeting progresses

After the Meeting

  • Access the full recording and transcript in your Fathom library
  • Review AI-generated highlights and summary
  • Share specific clips or the entire recording with team members
  • Export notes to your preferred tools (Notion, Slack, etc.)

Questions & Answers

Here are some common questions about using Fathom Notetaker in a Windows Team environment:

Is Fathom compatible with all Windows versions?

Fathom is compatible with Windows 10 and Windows 11. For older versions, check the system requirements on the Fathom website.

Can I use Fathom with both Microsoft Teams and Zoom?

Yes, Fathom works with multiple video conferencing platforms including Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, and Webex.

How secure is my meeting data with Fathom?

Fathom uses enterprise-grade security with encryption in transit and at rest. They also comply with privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA.

Can I customize the AI-generated summaries?

Yes, you can edit and customize the AI-generated summaries to better suit your team's needs.

Is there a limit to how many meetings I can record?

The free plan has limitations on the number of recordings and storage. Paid plans offer increased limits and additional features.
